What are the daily challenges?

Daily Challenges are fun, exclusive activities for Ipsos iSay members. While they all offer a delightful break, some might come with the added bonus of extra points, helping you get to your next reward even faster.

What is the Daily Prize Wheel?

The Daily Prize Wheel is one of our daily challenges and your ticket to excitement with Ipsos iSay! Every day, you get a chance to spin the wheel for a shot at winning fantastic prizes.
